About the Resort

Escape to an award-winning tropical paradise at The Horizon Glass Resort, Maldives. Nested on the private island of Vabbinfaru, this stunning resort features beautiful eco-villas with floor-to-ceiling glass panel windows, allowing for uninterrupted 360-degree views of pristine beaches, lush gardens, and crystal-clear turquoise waters.

Indulge in organic spa treatments, fine ocean dining, and explore rich coral marine life right off your private deck. Whether seeking a romantic honeymoon getaway or a quiet island sanctuary, Horizon Glass offers an unrivaled standard of premium barefoot luxury.
